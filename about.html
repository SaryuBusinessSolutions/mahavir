About Mahavir Diagnostic Centre & Interventional Radiology Clinic

Mahavir Diagnostic Centre & Interventional Radiology Clinic is a centre run by Jain doctors with latest diagnostic equipment that offers radiology services managed and headed by radiologist & pathologist who report the scans blood tests themselves and interact with you AND YOUR DOCTOR to facilitate quick treatment and management.
Being a centre owned and run by the doctor themselves they know the importance of accurate and fast diagnosis with quick reports to the patient. As regards to corporate our work is ethical and focussed entirely on providing optimal diagnosis with sympathy towards our patient.
Here the patients get opportunity to interact and discuss the reports with the doctors for clearer insight regarding their illness.
